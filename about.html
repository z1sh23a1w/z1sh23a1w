About Us

Our Mission

To enhance the student experience by providing essential services, fostering engagement, and ensuring a supportive environment. We serve as an active bridge between students and the college administration.

Through strategic collaboration with various councils and societies, we create inclusive experiences that foster a strong sense of belonging and meaningful community engagement throughout the academic year.

Representation

Organizing intake orientations and activities to help students transition smoothly into college life and feel at home within the campus.

Development

Guiding students to meet eligibility criteria for the ALSTAR Certificate through active participation and holistic development opportunities.

Welfare

Serving as a vital link between the student body and college administration for all concerns, ensuring your voice is always heard clearly.

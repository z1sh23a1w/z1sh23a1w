Core Structure

Departments

Leadership Development

Leadership Development promotes soft skills and sports to foster interpersonal growth and ensure all A-Level students develop personally and professionally.

Community Service

Community Service, or COMSERVE for short, organises events for our members that focus on charitable, environmental, or community-orientated work.

Student Welfare

Student Welfare strives to foster a supportive and holistic environment within the community by curating initiatives that enhance students’ mental, social, and academic well-being.

Public Relations

Public Relations focuses on creating creative and engaging content to communicate effectively and build a strong, connected A-Level community.
